Abacus,
A device that changed the world

Abacus, the first computing device made by humans.

Time line of abacus:-

  • 2700BC- The first appearance of the Sumerian abacus.

  • 600BC-Around 600 BC the Persians first began to use the abacus.

  • 500BC- the use of the Greek abacus dates to the 5th century BC.

  • 200BC-The earliest known written documentation of the Chinese abacus dates to the 2nd century BC.

  • 100-Romans stared using it from 1st century AD.

  • 300-Indian mathematcians started using it around 3rd century AD.

  • 1300-In Japan abacus is imported from China in the 14th century.

  • 1400-The Chinese abacus migrated from China to Korea around 1400 AD. Later it also enterd Russia.

  • 1820-The Russian abacus was brought to France around 1820.
